Top Books of Kannada according to me

My Best 3 Books

Jugari Cross

A captivating novel by Poornachandra Tejaswi.

The story unfolds within a 24hr timeframe,creating a sense of urgency and suspense

Mookajjiya Kanasugalu

One of The Jnanapeetha awarded novels written by K Shivarama Karanta

The story revolves around an elderly widow named Mookajji who possesses supernatural abilities to see the past through touching objects

Bettada Jeeva

A Beautiful story by K Shivarama Karanta

The story takes place in a remote village in the Western Ghats mountains
